# 1.3.2 — See Which Stops Are Upcoming vs Completed

**Epic:** Trip Management
**Feature:** Stop Progress

## User Story

As a user, I want to see which stops are upcoming vs completed so that I can understand where I am in my roadtrip at a glance.

## Description

The trip view visually distinguishes between stops already visited, the current stop, and upcoming stops. This gives the user an immediate sense of progress.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Departed stops are visually distinct (e.g., greyed out, checkmark, strikethrough).
- The current stop is highlighted or emphasised.
- Upcoming stops are shown in their default style.
- The overall trip progress is clear at a glance (e.g., "3 of 6 stops completed").

## Technical Notes

- Derives directly from the `status` field on each `Stop` (see 1.3.1).
- Consider a progress indicator (e.g., a vertical timeline with filled/empty dots) that works well on both phone and, later, the constrained Android Auto templated UI.
